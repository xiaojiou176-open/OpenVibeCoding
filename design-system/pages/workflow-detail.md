# Workflow Detail Override

This page is the **durable case record**.

## Primary job

Make it obvious:

- what the case is
- whether it can resume
- what queue/SLA posture it has
- what the next operator move is

## Rules

- The case identity and current verdict must be visually dominant.
- Queue actions and workflow copilot belong near the summary, not buried low.
- Related runs should support fast navigation into proof and compare.

## Avoid

- review/rating/product-detail tropes
- decorative trust badges
- copy that reads like a purchase journey
